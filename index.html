Overview

LeverLess is an app designed to help cryptocurrency leverage trading addicts recover. We take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Core Commitment: All your data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, upload, or share any of your personal information.

Data Storage

This app uses completely local data storage:

  • Recovery start date - Stored locally on your device
  • Daily loss amount settings - Stored locally on your device
  • Emotion journal entries - Stored locally on your device
  • Language and currency preferences - Stored locally on your device

All data is stored using the Hive local database and never leaves your device.

Permissions

This app may request the following permissions:

  • Notification Permission - Used to send daily encouragement reminders to help you stay motivated in your recovery. You can disable this at any time in Settings.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Location information
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage behavior data
  • Any financial information

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by:

  • Selecting "Reset All Data" in the app Settings
  • Uninstalling the app

Policy Updates

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the new version on this page. We recommend checking this page periodically.
